Weaknesses

Introduction Strengths Weaknesses

In some situations Bogdan’s lateral movement is not good as it should be. He can’t react fast enough to stop players in certain situations.

Bogdan is prone to injuries due to his aggressiveness and mentality. He endured 5 injuries during his 4 years at LCC Moose team.

Bogdan’s percentages off the dribble are not as good as they should be. He should put more work in this area.

In addition to the areas mentioned above, another weakness in Bogdan's game lies in his shot selection. While he possesses a commendable shooting ability, there are instances where he tends to take ill-advised shots, particularly when under defensive pressure. This impulsive decision-making can lead to a lower shooting efficiency and provide opponents with opportunities to capitalize on offensive rebounds.
